Weber Counseling Services

Our mission is to provide quality service in an environment that fosters your ability to resolve and bring change in order to help achieve and attain your goals in therapy and life.

We believe in client centered therapy for empowering clients in developing healthy coping skills and breaking generational trauma.

What We Do

Our clinicians are trained in the following therapeutic models:

  • Interpersonal Therapy
  •   Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  •   Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing (EMDR)
  •   Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT)
  •   Attachement Therapy
  •   Behavioral Therapy
  •   Mindfulness
  •   Play Therapy
  •   Solution Focus Brief Therapy (SFBT)
  •   Motivational Interviewing
  •   Gottman Method
  •   Trauma Therapy
  •   Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
